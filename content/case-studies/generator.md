# Storycraft: AI Case Study Generator

## Challenge

After wrapping a project, the last thing you want to do is spend half a day writing about it. So it gets skipped. Then the next project gets skipped. The portfolio stays thin, and new clients have less to evaluate.

The alternatives don't help much. Handing ChatGPT a bullet list produces inconsistent output that still needs editing. Hiring a writer costs $50–200 and takes 3–5 days. The bottleneck isn't skill — it's friction.

## Solution

Answer 3–4 questions: what was the client's problem, what did you build, what changed. Storycraft expands those answers into a structured case study, in your voice, with consistent formatting throughout.

Export to Markdown, HTML, or PDF. Done.

## Results

**3–6 hours → 10–15 minutes** per case study, every time.

**Consistent structure and tone** across all projects — no revision cycles to make them feel like they came from the same studio.

**Exports directly** to Markdown, HTML, or PDF — no reformatting work.

## Built because we needed it

We built Storycraft for ourselves. It's what powers the case studies on this site. The questions are designed the way they are not by guessing what works, but by going through the process dozens of times.
