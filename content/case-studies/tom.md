# TOM: Meeting Intelligence That Compounds

## Challenge

You took notes. Maybe you got an auto-summary. Three months later you need to remember what was decided in that call with that client, and you have nowhere to look except a folder of recordings you're not going to sit through.

Most meeting tools solve one meeting at a time. Record, transcribe, summarize, done. None of them help with the harder problem: thinking across dozens of meetings and making actual use of what was decided.

## Solution

TOM records using Apple's ScreenCaptureKit — no bot joins the call, nothing leaves your device. Transcription runs through your own OpenAI key. The data stays on your Mac.

After each call: a summary with action items, decisions, TL;DR. That's the baseline. The real thing is cross-meeting search — ask a question across your entire meeting history and get cited answers with timestamps pointing back to the exact moment in the video.

Month 1 is useful. Month 6 is a different tool entirely.

## Results

**Search across your entire meeting history** Not just the last call — every decision, every commitment, every context point, going back as far as you've been recording.

**No privacy tradeoffs** No bot in the call. No data sent to our servers. Your OpenAI key means we never see your transcripts.

## Two studios, one problem

TOM started as an internal fix. Both Optimotion and [Automatics](https://www.automatics.dev) were running into the same wall — meetings happening, decisions made, nothing findable three weeks later.

We built it for ourselves first. It's still what we both use daily.

- Your OpenAI key, your data — we never see it
- Auto summaries after every call: action items, decisions, TL;DR
- Cross-meeting Q&A with citations and timestamps
