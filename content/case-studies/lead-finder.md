# Lead Finder: Location & ICP-Based Prospecting Tool

## Challenge

Finding qualified leads manually takes a disproportionate amount of time. You know who you're looking for — the sector, the size, the location — but getting from that description to an actual list of contacts means a lot of searching, checking, and filtering before a single message goes out.

It gets worse in new markets. No existing network means starting from zero.

## Solution

Define three things: sector, location, and ICP criteria. What does a good-fit prospect actually look like for you? Lead Finder runs the search and returns contacts that match.

No manual trawling. No building lists by hand.

## Results

**No more list-building from scratch** The slow, manual phase before outreach is eliminated. Define your ICP once, get a matched contact list back.

**Market entry without local knowledge** Our Germany expansion runs on Lead Finder. Our channel partner uses it to find prospects in an unfamiliar market without needing to know who's who locally.

## What we use it for

Optimotion's own outbound pipeline runs on this. It's also what we built for our Germany expansion — our channel partner there uses it to find prospects without needing local knowledge of who's who.

Less time searching. More time actually reaching out.
